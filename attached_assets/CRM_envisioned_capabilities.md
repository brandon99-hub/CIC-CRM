# CRM Envisioned Capabilities

---

## 1. 360-Degree Stakeholder Profile Management

The CRM should create a single unified stakeholder profile for every interaction across all channels.

### Stakeholders Covered

- Students/candidates
- Alumni
- Training institutions
- Employers
- Corporate partners
- Government agencies
- Media
- Sponsors
- Regional/international students
- Suppliers/vendors
- Staff members

### Unified Profile

Each stakeholder profile should include:

- Personal details
- Qualification pathway
- Registration history
- Examination history
- Payment history
- Complaints and queries
- Communication history
- Certificates awarded
- Institution attached to
- Social engagement history
- CRM interaction score
- Region/country/county

### Benefits

- Staff see complete stakeholder history instantly
- Faster issue resolution
- Personalized communication
- Better relationship management

## 2. Omnichannel Contact Centre Integration

This is one of the most critical features. The CRM should integrate all communication channels into one dashboard.

### Channels

- Phone calls
- Email
- WhatsApp
- Live chat
- Chatbot
- Social media
- Website inquiries
- Walk-ins
- SMS

### Smart Ticketing

Every inquiry automatically generates a ticket with:

- Unique reference number
- Priority level
- SLA tracking
- Assigned officer
- Escalation timelines

### Automatic Routing

Queries routed automatically based on:

- Topic
- Department
- Region
- Priority
- Stakeholder category

### Call Logging

- Automatic recording
- Notes
- Sentiment analysis
- Quality assurance scoring

### WhatsApp Integration

Stakeholders should be able to:

- Ask questions
- Receive reminders
- Download documents
- Track tickets
- Receive results notifications
